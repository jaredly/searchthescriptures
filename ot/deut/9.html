THE FIFTH BOOK OF MOSES CALLED
DEUTERONOMY
CHAPTER 9
Other nations driven out of Canaan because of their wickedness—Moses rehearses rebellions of Israel, and tells how he mediated between them and the Lord—On two occasions he has gone without food and water for forty days.
