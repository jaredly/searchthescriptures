THE FIRST EPISTLE OF PAUL THE APOSTLE TO THE
CORINTHIANS
CHAPTER 6
Brethren should not go to law with each other—The unrighteous shall not be saved—True saints are the temple of the Holy Ghost.

  5 I speak to your ashame. Is it so, that there is not a wise man among you? no, not one that shall be able to judge between his brethren?
