THE GOSPEL ACCORDING TO
ST MATTHEW
CHAPTER 21
Jesus rides in triumph into Jerusalem—He cleanses the temple, curses the fig tree, and discusses authority—He gives the parables of the two sons, and the wicked husbandmen.
