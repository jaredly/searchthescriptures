PSALM 127
Children are an heritage from the Lord.
A SONG OF DEGREES FOR SOLOMON.
  1 Except the Lord build the house, they alabour in vain that build it: except the Lord bkeep the city, the watchman waketh but in vain.
  2 It is vain for you to rise up early, to sit up late, to eat the bread of sorrows: for so he giveth his beloved asleep.
  3 Lo, achildren are an bheritage of the Lord: and the fruit of the womb is his creward.
  4 As arrows are in the hand of a mighty man; so are children of the youth.
  5 aHappy is the man that hath his quiver full of them: they shall not be ashamed, but they shall speak with the enemies in the gate.
