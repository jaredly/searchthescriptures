CHAPTER 5
Inhabitants of Canaan fear Israel—Males of Israel are circumcised—Israel keeps the passover, eats fruit of land, and manna ceases—Captain of the Lord’s host appears to Joshua.

  5 Now all the people that came out were circumcised: but all the people that were born in the wilderness by the way as they came forth out of Egypt, them they had not circumcised.
